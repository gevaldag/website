Our Heritage

Tradition meets boutique craftsmanship.

At Gevaldag, we believe that a true Shabbos or Yom Tov table isn't complete without the perfect slice of herring. Born in Jerusalem out of a passion for authentic Jewish culinary traditions, we've elevated a classic staple into a gourmet experience.

We source only the fattest, premium catches, curing them with secret family blends of spices, fresh onions, premium oils, and select ingredients. No shortcuts, just pure, melt-in-your-mouth perfection. A highlight is our signature 4-flavor platter, perfect for any gathering.
